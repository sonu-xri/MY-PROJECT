❓ Help & User Guide

Welcome to the 📚 Notes & PYQ SHARING! This platform is designed to help students share and access study materials easily. Here you can upload your notes, download previous year question papers, and collaborate with other students.

📖 The Notes section allows you to explore subject-wise materials uploaded by students and educators. Each note includes ratings, download options, and sharing features to improve accessibility.

📄 In the PYQ section, you can find previous year question papers that help you understand exam patterns and improve your preparation strategy.

📤 The Upload feature allows you to contribute your own study materials. You can upload files in PDF, Word, PPT, or Image format. Make sure your file size is under 500MB.

⭐ The rating system helps users identify high-quality content. You can rate materials based on usefulness.

🔗 Share feature allows you to easily share notes with your friends using links or social platforms.

⚠️ Please ensure that you upload only educational and relevant content. Any misuse of the platform may result in account restrictions.

📞 If you face any issues or need assistance, feel free to contact us through the Contact page. We are always here to help you succeed in your academic journey.

🚀 Keep learning, keep sharing, and grow together!
